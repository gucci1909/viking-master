    Frequently Asked Questions
    Question: What does the Foot Operated Faucet do?
    Answer: We normally open and close the faucet with hands; the same function can be performed using foot through a pedal as per the unique design of the Foot Operated Faucet.
    Question: Why the foot-operated faucet (tap)?
    Answer: Cross-contamination is one of the biggest threats to us today and solely to combat that problem, the foot pedal faucet has been designed. This tap helps mitigate the challenges of virus contamination and thus protects us from multiple infections.
    Question: How to avoid cross-contamination through foot-operated faucet?
    Answer: The unique design proves to be an extremely efficient hands-free tap that helps prevent the spread of infections. With its compact design, pocket-friendly cost, and convenience of controlling the flow of water, this faucet not only fits anywhere but helps manage cost and but save water as well.
    Question: How does this faucet act as a water-saving solution?
    Answer: Hand-operated taps give rise to one problem, and that is the running of flow of water while your hands are busy like during shaving, brushing, trimming, while in the kitchen or in almost all public places where people hesitate from touching the tap for fear of contamination. The foot-operated faucet proves to be the perfect solution to such challenges. The sophisticated design of this tap helps control the flow of water simply through your foot. A simple nudge of the foot and the flow of water from the faucet can be increased or decreased making this faucet one of the most effective water-saving solutions for bathrooms and public spaces.
    Question: Where can the Foot-operated faucet be used?
    Answer: As far as the design and aesthetics of the faucet are concerned, it is suitable for use at almost any place. But since the main purpose of this design is to prevent cross-contamination, the use of this imperative in spaces where the maintaining hygiene is of utmost significance like Hospitals, Health Care Facilities, Pharmaceutical factories, Food processing (production) units, and even in the kitchen. Further, taking the current situation into account the use of this faucet has become much more obligatory in common public places like Restaurants, Cinemas, Shopping Malls, Public Toilets, Government/Private offices, Shops, and Showrooms, Factories, Train / Bus Stations and Airports.
    Question: Why is the Foot Operated Faucet Design from Viking the best Foot Faucet in 2020?
    Answer: A. The Compact design of this faucet makes it suitable for any place. It can fit in small bathrooms and even under Vanity (a place not accessible by many foot faucets).                                     B. As per the design from Viking, The pedal of this faucet has flow control, a unique feature that is not available in most products in the market.                                                                                                 C. This design is incorporated with an extremely convenient pedal to press and release. The pedal is only 25mm above floor this pedal is flappable too, making the cleaning of the floor extremely convenient.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         D. Many foot faucets work with push type not press type design which implies no flow control (either on or off through the pedal), but this foot faucet design help manage the flow of water as well.
    Question: Does the use of this faucet lead to any other problems?
    Answer: This design has been assembled keeping in mind the beautiful design of your flooring, therefore no,the pedal on this faucet does not scratch the floor upon the full press. And as for any other challenges, this faucet is just like the traditional hand-operated faucet, if anything else this design is more practical and comfortable to use leading to no other problems.
    Question: Is the design of this faucet compatible with Hot water?
    Answer: Yes, this design works perfectly with hot water as well. A simple installation of an additional valve before the water inlet to the tap and you are good to go with hot water.
    Question: What is the Warranty offered on this faucet?
    Answer: With complete faith in this design, Viking offers a 3 years warranty on this foot-operated faucet design.
    Question: How do you fix a leaking or dripping Foot faucet (tap)?
    Answer: There is no need to change the whole equipment in case of a leaking or dripping foot faucet rather an easily replaceable spindle is the solution. This spindle can easily be replaced with a new one by a layman too.
